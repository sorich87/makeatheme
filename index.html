Frequently Asked Questions

How it works?

Scroll down the page to see the full list of all the themes and choose the one you like. The themes are ordered by popularity, but there maybe an hidden gem below.
When you find the theme you want, click the "Customize" button and you will be taken to the customizer where you can make edits until you are satisfied.
Then, click on "Buy Now" to place your order. You will get a link to download the theme with your customizations but you can also request us to install it on your site for you.

Something doesn't work. What should I do?

It maybe our fault and we are sorry for that. This site is a work in progress and we are building new features and fixing bugs every day. Please contact us if something doesn't work for you and we will quickly find a solution.

Do I get support and updates for the themes I buy?

Yes, you get lifetime support and updates. When there is a new version of the theme, we will notify you in your website dashboard so that you update your theme right from there.
